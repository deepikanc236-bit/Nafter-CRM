Frequently Asked Questions

What digital services does Nafter Web Technologies offer?
We specialize in Web Development, Shopify E-commerce solutions, SEO Optimization, Digital Marketing, Branding Strategy, and Custom Software Development to help businesses grow online.
How can SEO help my business grow?
Our expert SEO strategies improve your website's ranking on search engines like Google, driving organic traffic, increasing visibility, and leading to higher conversion rates without paid ads.
Do you build custom e-commerce stores using Shopify?
Yes, we are Shopify experts. We create custom, responsive, and high-converting Shopify stores tailored to your brand's specific needs, ensuring a smooth shopping experience for your customers.
How does your digital marketing approach differ?
We use a data-driven approach, combining market research, competitor analysis, and creative content to build targeted campaigns that deliver real ROI, not just clicks.
What is the typical timeline for a website project?
The timeline varies by project complexity. A standard business website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce or custom web apps may take 6-10 weeks. We provide a detailed schedule during consultation.
Do you provide support after the website is launched?
Absolutely. We offer ongoing maintenance, technical support, and security updates to ensure your website remains fast, secure, and up-to-date.
Do you develop mobile applications for Android and iOS?
Yes! We build high-performance native and cross-platform mobile apps using modern frameworks like Flutter and React Native to extend your business to mobile users.
My current website is outdated. Can you redesign it?
Definitely. We can modernize your existing website with a fresh design, better user experience (UX), mobile responsiveness, and improved SEO structure to boost its performance.
Do you provide content writing services?
Yes, our team of professional copywriters can create engaging, SEO-optimized content for your website, blogs, and marketing materials that resonates with your target audience.
Is the initial consultation free?
Yes, we offer a completely free initial consultation to discuss your project requirements, goals, and how we can help you achieve them. Book a call with us today!
